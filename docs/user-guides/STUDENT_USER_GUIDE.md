## 1. Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Open your browser and go to your college's EduNexus URL
2. Click "Sign In"
3. Enter your college email and password
4. Complete verification if prompted

### First-Time Setup
1. Update your profile with photo and contact details
2. Verify your personal information
3. Add emergency contact details
4. Review your academic information

### Mobile Access
EduNexus works on any device! For the best mobile experience:
- Add the app to your home screen
- Enable notifications for important updates
- The app works offline for basic features

## 4. Attendance

### Viewing Attendance
1. Go to **Attendance** from the menu
2. See overall attendance percentage
3. View subject-wise breakdown
4. Check monthly attendance calendar

### Understanding Attendance Colors
- **Green**: Present
- **Red**: Absent
- **Yellow**: Late
- **Blue**: On Duty / Leave

### Attendance Alerts
You'll receive alerts when:
- Attendance falls below 75%
- Multiple consecutive absences
- Risk of shortage

### Requesting Leave
1. Go to **Attendance** > **Apply Leave**
2. Select leave type and dates
3. Provide reason and documents
4. Submit for approval

## 5. Exams & Results

### Exam Schedule
1. Go to **Exams** from menu
2. View upcoming exams
3. See exam details (date, time, venue)
4. Add to calendar

### Viewing Results
1. Go to **Exams** > **Results**
2. Select semester
3. View subject-wise marks and grades
4. Download marksheet

### Understanding Grades

| Grade | Points | Percentage |
|-------|--------|------------|
| A+ | 10 | 90-100% |
| A | 9 | 80-89% |
| B+ | 8 | 70-79% |
| B | 7 | 60-69% |
| C | 6 | 50-59% |
| D | 4 | 40-49% |
| F | 0 | Below 40% |

### CGPA/SGPA
- **SGPA**: Semester Grade Point Average
- **CGPA**: Cumulative Grade Point Average
- Both are calculated automatically based on your grades

## 6. Fees & Payments

### Viewing Fee Structure
1. Go to **Fees** from menu
2. See all fee components
3. View payment history
4. Download receipts

### Making Payment
1. Go to **Fees** > **Pay Now**
2. Select fees to pay
3. Choose payment method:
   - UPI
   - Credit/Debit Card
   - Net Banking
4. Complete payment
5. Download receipt

### Payment History
- View all past payments
- Download receipts
- Check pending dues
- View payment deadlines

## 8. Library

### Browsing Catalog
1. Go to **Library** from menu
2. Search by title, author, or subject
3. Check availability
4. Reserve if not available

### Borrowing Books
1. Go to library with your ID
2. Show book code to librarian
3. Book issued to your card
4. Return before due date

### E-Resources
1. Go to **Library** > **E-Resources**
2. Browse digital content
3. Access journals and papers
4. Download for offline reading

### Fines
- Late returns: Rs. 5/day
- Lost books: Full replacement cost
- Check fine status in app

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page and follow instructions.

**Q: Can I change my profile photo?**
A: Yes, go to Profile > Edit and upload a new photo.

**Q: How do I download fee receipt?**
A: Go to Fees > Payment History > Click on transaction > Download.

**Q: Who do I contact for technical issues?**
A: Raise a support ticket or email helpdesk@college.edu.
