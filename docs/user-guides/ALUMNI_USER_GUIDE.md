# Alumni User Guide

Welcome to the EduNexus Alumni Portal. This guide will help you navigate all the features available to you as a valued alumni of the institution.

## Getting Started

### Logging In
1. Visit the college portal URL (e.g., `https://nexus-ec.edunexus.io`)
2. Click "Sign In" in the top navigation
3. Enter your credentials (email: `alumni@{college-domain}.edu`)
4. You'll be redirected to your Alumni Dashboard

### First-Time Setup
If you're a new alumni registering:
1. Click "Alumni Registration" on the homepage
2. Fill in your graduation details and current employment
3. Submit for verification by admin
4. Once approved, you'll receive login credentials

## Profile Management

### Updating Your Profile

Navigate to **Profile** in the sidebar.

**Personal Information:**
- Name, email, phone
- LinkedIn, Twitter, website URLs
- Profile photo

**Academic Details:**
- Graduation year and batch
- Department and degree
- Final CGPA
- Achievements during college

**Privacy Settings:**
- Show/hide email in directory
- Show/hide phone number
- Show/hide employment details

### Employment History

Track your career journey:

1. Go to **Profile > Employment History**
2. Click **Add Employment**
3. Fill in:
   - Company name and role
   - Location
   - Start date and end date (leave blank if current)
   - Industry and company size
   - Brief description

**Tips:**
- Keep your employment updated for accurate directory listings
- Verified employment is more visible to students

## Mentorship

One of the most impactful ways to give back is by mentoring current students.

### Enabling Mentorship

1. Go to **Settings > Mentorship Preferences**
2. Toggle "Open to Mentoring" to ON
3. Select your mentorship areas:
   - Career guidance
   - Technical mentoring
   - Interview preparation
   - Resume review
   - Project guidance
   - Industry insights

### Managing Mentorship Requests

Students can request mentorship based on your profile.

1. Go to **Mentorship > Requests**
2. View pending requests with:
   - Student name and batch
   - Area of interest
   - Request message
3. Click **Accept** or **Decline** (provide reason)

### Active Mentorships

For accepted mentorships:

1. Go to **Mentorship > Active**
2. View your mentees
3. For each mentee:
   - Schedule meetings
   - Log meeting notes
   - Track progress

### Completing a Mentorship

When the mentorship is complete:

1. Click **Complete Mentorship**
2. Rate your mentee (1-5)
3. Provide a brief review
4. The student will also rate the mentorship

## FAQ

**Q: How do I update my graduation details?**
A: Contact the admin as graduation details are verified and locked.

**Q: Can I hide my profile from the directory?**
A: Yes, go to Settings > Privacy and disable "Visible in Directory."

**Q: How many mentees can I accept?**
A: There's no limit, but we recommend managing 2-3 active mentorships at a time.

**Q: Are my donation amounts visible?**
A: By default, only the fact that you contributed is visible. You can choose to make amounts public.
