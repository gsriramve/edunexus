## Video Production Guide

### Recommended Specs
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (Full HD)
- **Duration**: 3-4 minutes
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)

### Style Guide
- **Transitions**: Smooth cross-dissolve (0.5s) between screens
- **Pacing**: 2-3 seconds pause on each key screen
- **Lower thirds**: Show persona name as text overlay
- **Music**: Upbeat, corporate-friendly (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)

### Tools
| Purpose | Tool |
|---------|------|
| Screen Recording | Loom, OBS Studio, or QuickTime |
| Video Editing | DaVinci Resolve (free), Adobe Premiere |
| Voiceover | AI voice (ElevenLabs) or professional VO |
| Music | YouTube Audio Library (free) |

## Quick Video Creation Options

### Option 1: Slideshow Video (Fastest)
1. Import all 12 PNGs into Canva or Google Slides

3. Export as video with music

### Option 2: Screen Recording (Most Authentic)

3. Edit in DaVinci Resolve
